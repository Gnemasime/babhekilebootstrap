About the Founder

A Vision Built on
Two Decades of Impact

Lindiwe Twala's journey began in 2002 with a simple conviction: that skills and knowledge are the foundation of meaningful transformation. What started as a small computer training centre has evolved into one of South Africa's trusted transformation advisory firms.

Over 24 years, Lindiwe has worked across corporates, government, NGOs and development institutions — gaining deep insight into how transformation truly happens. She has seen what works and what doesn't, and built Babhekile on the lessons of real-world experience.

"Transformation must be practical, structured, and accountable. Strategy means nothing if it doesn't translate into real impact for real people."

"Babhekile exists to bridge the gap between what organisations plan and what they actually deliver."
